Individual development plans to track goals, log training, and celebrate progress. Turn informal chats into actionable growth plans.

Coffee & Cake Chats

A dedicated space to schedule and log notes from your informal 1:1s, helping you keep track of individual ambitions and concerns.

Achievement Log

A timeline of individual accomplishments. Provides concrete examples for performance reviews and makes your team feel valued.

A digital noticeboard for peer-to-peer recognition. Fosters a positive environment where the team celebrates each other's success.

A quick, anonymous emoji poll for the team to rate their week. Get a real-time gauge of team morale and spot potential issues early.

Exit Interviews

A professional, structured form for conducting and logging interviews with departing employees to capture invaluable, honest feedback.
